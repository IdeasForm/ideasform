## What is mcalendar?

mcalendar is a small calendar app for macOS that helps you quickly check the date and your calendar information with a lightweight interface.

## Do I need to create an account?

No. mcalendar does not require a separate account to use the app.

## Does mcalendar collect my data?

No. mcalendar does not collect personal information, analytics data, or tracking data from you.

## Is my calendar data sent to your servers?

No. If you allow mcalendar to access calendar information on your Mac, that data stays on your device and is not sent to our servers.
