## What is Inline?

Inline is an intelligent assistant app that brings AI-powered chat capabilities directly to your workflow. It provides instant AI assistance without leaving your current context, helping you get help with whatever you're working on without switching apps.

## How do I use Inline?

There are a few ways to start a chat with Inline:
- **From the menubar:** Click the Inline icon in your menubar to open the chat panel.
- **With a text selection:** Select any text on your screen and press your configured shortcut to instantly start a chat with that content.
- **With screen capture:** Use the selection tool to capture a portion of your screen. Inline will use OCR to extract the text and begin a chat.

## How much does Inline cost?

Inline offers a free tier with 7 complimentary requests every day. If you need more, you can purchase an additional 200 requests for chat.

## How do I purchase more requests?

When you've used your daily free quota, the app will provide an option to purchase more requests. Simply follow the on-screen instructions to complete your purchase through the App Store.
